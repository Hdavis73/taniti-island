Local Transportation

Getting around in a new place can be daunting, but Taniti makes it easy!

Taniti City Public Bus

6am - 11pm

Taniti City's main form of transportation option is public buses. These buses will get you where you need to go in a timely manner and run from 6am - 11pm every day.

Private Bus

The rest of the city is served by public buses, the their schedule is dependent on which service you use.

View Schedule
Flysten, N. (2019). Photo. Unsplash. https://unsplash.com/photos/xQkAk3yTUFM

Walking

Taniti City is fairly flat and very walkable. Many tourists stay in the area surrounding Merriton Landing: this area is easy to explore on foot.

Nix, T. (2018). Photo. Unsplash. https://unsplash.com/photos/VZEj0iepzKA

Car or Bike Rental

Cars can be rented from a local rental agency located near the airport for your convenience. If you're on a budget, bikes and helmets are available for rent throughout the island, but keep in mind, helments are required by law!

Ready For Your Trip?

Now that you're ready for the vacation of a life time, all you need to do is book your travel! Below are the travel options our visitors take to get to us.

Airplane

Taniti is served by a small airport that can accommodate small jets and propeller planes. This is the way most of our visitors reach us.

Book A Flight

Cruise Ship

Some guests arrive on a small cruise ship that docks in Yellow Leaf Bay for one night per week. If you're looking for a short visit to multiple islands, this may be the option for you!
