How To Get Around

Local Transportation

Getting around in a new place can be daunting, but Taniti makes it easy!

Taniti City Public Bus

5am - 11pm

Taniti City's main form of transportation option is public buses. These buses will get you where you need to go in a timely manner and run from 6am - 11pm every day.

Private Bus

The rest of the city is served by public buses, the their schedule is dependent on which service you use.

View Schedule

Walking

Taniti City is fairly flat and very walkable. Many tourists stay in the area surrounding Merriton Landing: this area is easy to explore on foot.

Car or Bike Rental

Cars can be rented from a local rental agency located near the airport for your convenience. If you're on a budget, bikes and helmets are available for rent throughout the island, but keep in mind, helments are required by law!
